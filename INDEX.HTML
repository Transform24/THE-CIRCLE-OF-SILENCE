Is This For You?

The Circle calls those who are ready

I · The Seeker

You have tried meditation before. Apps, YouTube, free guides. Something always felt missing. A depth. A real container. Someone who takes it as seriously as you want to.

II · The Healer

You are carrying something — grief, transition, burnout, spiritual hunger — and you know that the answer lives in the silence. You just need someone to hold the space while you go there.

III · The Devoted

You are not looking for a hobby. You are looking for a practice. Something that asks something of you. A daily discipline. A community. Real structure.

IV · The Global Soul

You are in Lagos, London, Los Angeles, Mumbai — it does not matter. The silence has no time zone. This ministry was built to meet you in your morning, wherever that morning is.

If you are here because something called you — that is enough. You belong in this circle.

The Structure

Three Phases. One Unfolding.

Every day of your journey has a purpose. Every phase builds on the last. This is not a playlist. It is a path.

I

The Awakening

Coming Home to Stillness

We begin in the body. In the breath. In the radical act of simply being present with what is. Daily guided meditations. Breathing rituals. Your first real meeting with silence.

Days 1–30 · Available in 30-day path
II

The Deepening

Meeting What Has Been Hidden

True transformation requires courage. We descend — with reverence, with support — into the patterns, the grief, the shadow. This is where the real work begins.

Days 31–60 · 90-day path only
III

The Embodiment

Living What You Have Received

You do not complete a transformation. You become it. We weave everything into the body, the breath, the daily walk. You carry the circle back into your life.

Days 61–90 · 90-day path only

Your practice lives in the app — your breathing circles, your meditation timer, your sacred journal — available whenever your morning is.

Before You Enter

The Sacred Pledge

Every member of this circle reads these words before they join. This is not a contract. It is an intention.

I come to this circle with intention.

I commit to showing up — not perfectly, but honestly.

I will not rush the silence.
I will not abandon myself when it gets difficult.

I give myself 30 days of faithful practice — and if I am called deeper, 90.

I understand that transformation is not an event.
It is a way of walking.

The circle holds me.
I hold the circle.

— The Circle of Silence
